Thomas Michael ‘Tommy’ Shelby is the son of Arthur and Mrs. Shelby, brother of Arthur, John, Ada and Finn Shelby,
father of Charles and Ruby Shelby, as well as being the husband of Grace and Lizzie Shelby. He is the leader of the Birmingham
criminal gang, the Peaky Blinders and the patriarch of the Shelby Family.
